I am a fourth-year CS Ph.D. student at Stanford University. I am a member of Stanford Pervasive Parallelism Lab and LMCache. My advisor is Kunle Olukotun. I have also been working with James Zou, Junchen Jiang, and Muhammad Shahbaz. I obtained my Bachelor's degree from the University of Chicago.
